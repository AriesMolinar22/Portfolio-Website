Web design

Web design refers to the process of creating a visual and functional interface for websites or web applications. It involves the use of various design elements such as layout, typography, colors, graphics, and images to create an aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly website.

Video Editing

Video editing refers to the process of manipulating and rearranging video footage to create a final product that tells a story or conveys a message. It involves using specialized software to trim, cut, splice, and arrange video clips, as well as add transitions, visual effects, music, and sound effects.

2D Animation

2D animation is a traditional animation technique that involves creating a series of individual drawings, also known as frames, that are played back in sequence to create the illusion of movement. It is one of the oldest and most popular animation techniques, widely used in television shows, movies, commercials, and video games.
